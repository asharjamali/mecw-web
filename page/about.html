Mewat Engineering College is the pioneer college in the field of Engineering in India. With the vision to provide excellence in technical education, meet the growing demands of industries in the future and benefit the society as a whole. It is a full fledged college imparting full time programme B.Tech in Computer Science & Engineering, Electrical and Electronics Engineering, Electronics and Communications Engineering, Mechanical Engineering and Civil Engineering.The College aims at providing modern education alongside Islamic education to the minority community. In addition to running various schools, vocational institutions, providing grants to number of schools and colleges; the Board has established Mewat Engineering College (Waqf).

High tech Computer Lab- The College has high tech Computer Centre equipped with large number of PCs with multi-terminal facilities and management oriented software packages. Highly qualified faculty- The College has dynamic and dedicated faculty that are committed towards the goal oriented efficient teaching with effective methodology. Scholarship- The eligible students of Mewat Engineering College are given scholarship by various boards.
